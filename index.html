Skirt of the cards
Game difficulty
  • Easy(5*2)
  • Normal(6*3)
  • Hard(8*3)

How to play:

Put your memory to the test. Match the cards in as few moves as you can. Be sure to challenge yourself with a higher level if it's too easy.
When the game begins, all the pictures are hidden.
To play:

  1. Turn over any two cards.
  2. If the two cards match, they are going to disappear.
  3. If they don't match, they are turning back over.
  4. Remember what was on each card and where it was.
  5. The game is over when all the cards have been matched.
